Weekly Overview

As Spring Semester 2026 begins, recording my trash shows my habits. Through the pictures, you may be able to see my routine as a commuter working at a restaurant and an arcade, alongside my new DJing hobby. Items like crumpled aluminum foil and cleaning rags show the reality of food service, while a penguin plush and a Snoopy blindbox highlight my time at the arcade. A Hercules DJ controller box marks a new musical endeavor, and antifreeze reminds me of the maintenance required for the commuter lifestyle. Miscellaneous items like Lego packaging, a fortune cookie wrapper, and a restaurant receipt round out the week's detritus.
